Lasagna

This is a recipe for the finest, Italian lasagna. Enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Beef
  • Chicken Stock
  • Bechamel Sauce

Steps:

  1. Get baking tray
  2. Place Tomatoes
  3. Place Pasta
  4. Place Tomatoes
  5. Place Bechamel
  6. Repeat
  7. Into the oven for 10 hours @4300 Degrees C
